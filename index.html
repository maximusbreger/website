Biography

Maximus Breger is an eighteen-year-old pianist who performs with exceptional emotional intensity and technique. A prizewinner at the American Virtuoso International Music Competition, Maximus has been privileged to play at such venues as Carnegie Hall, delighting listeners with his performances that combine precise execution with the element of musical story-telling.

Having undergone training in classical piano under the mentorship of Dr. Margarita Loukachkina, Maximus combines a thorough knowledge of music and a distinct musical interpretation of compositions in all his recitals. He will be continuing his studies under Dr. Andrew Brownell of the University of Texas at Austin this fall.
